Subtracting Two Polynomials

Subtracting two polynomials involves subtracting the corresponding terms with the same degree. Here are the steps to subtract two polynomials:

  1. Arrange Polynomials: Align the polynomials vertically with like terms lined up.
  2. Subtract Like Terms: Subtract the coefficients of like terms from each other. If a term is missing in one polynomial, treat its coefficient as zero.
  3. Combine Terms: Combine like terms after subtraction to simplify the resulting polynomial.

By following these steps, you can effectively subtract one polynomial from another.
