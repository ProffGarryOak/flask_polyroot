Adding Two Polynomials

Adding two polynomials involves adding their respective terms with the same degree:

  1. Step 1: Align the like terms of the polynomials vertically, based on their degrees.
  2. Step 2: Add the coefficients of the like terms together.
  3. Step 3: Write down the sum of the coefficients as the coefficient of the resulting term.
  4. Step 4: Repeat the process for all terms with the same degree.
  5. Step 5: If one polynomial has terms with degrees not present in the other polynomial, simply copy those terms to the result.
  6. Step 6: Simplify the resulting polynomial by combining like terms.

By following these steps, you can add two polynomials to obtain their sum.
